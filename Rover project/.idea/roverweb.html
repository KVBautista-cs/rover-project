About Christian Brothers University

Christian Brothers University (CBU) is a private Roman Catholic university in Memphis, Tennessee. It is affiliated with the Institute of the Brothers of the Christian Schools, a Catholic teaching order, and is one of more than 1,000 Lasallian educational institutions in 77 countries.

Our team at CBU is dedicated to excellence in engineering and innovation, with a strong focus on hands-on learning and practical experience. We strive to apply our knowledge in meaningful ways, contributing to projects like the Rover, which enhances our skills and prepares us for future careers in the field.
